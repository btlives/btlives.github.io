What we do

Here at BTL we are determined to raise awareness to knife crime in London. We encourage everyone in the community to join us to make the streets of London a safer place. We can do this by coming together as a community and raise money for those who are victims of knife crime. The BTL platform also provides a solid platform where users can share their stories and how their experiences have affected their lives (positively/negatively). It is our job as a community to listen to those affected and work together to help them.
